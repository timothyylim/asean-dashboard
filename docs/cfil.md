# Climate Finance Innovation Lab (CFIL) Malaysia

- hosted by **Bank Pembangunan Malaysia Berhad (BPMB)** — Malaysia's development bank
- https://www.bpmb.com.my/mycfil/
- focus: climate finance innovation, connecting capital with climate projects
- structured programs with thematic areas and partner organisations

## What they do

- lab-style program that brings together climate projects seeking finance
- project showcases with company partnerships
- eligible programs across multiple thematic areas
- inquiry/application process for participation

## Why it matters

- central node in Malaysia's climate finance ecosystem
- connected to government-backed development finance
- good entry point for understanding who's doing what in Malaysia's climate space
